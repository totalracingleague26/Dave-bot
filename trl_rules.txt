# ğŸ“œ TRL League Rules & Regulations

-# These rules are in place to ensure **clean, fair, and competitive racing** for all drivers.
-# By racing in TRL, you agree to follow these regulations at all times.

---

## 1. Driver Conduct

-# 1.1 Respect all drivers, admins, and stewards at all times.
-# 1.2 No abusive language, harassment, or toxic behaviour in voice or text chat.
-# 1.3 Follow instructions from league officials.
-# 1.4 Unsportsmanlike behaviour may result in penalties or removal from the league.

---

## 2. General Racing Rules

-# 2.1 **Clean racing is mandatory.**
-# 2.2 Avoid contact whenever possible.
-# 2.3 Intentional contact or retaliation is strictly prohibited.
-# 2.4 Always attempt to leave racing room when cars are alongside.
-# 2.5 The car ahead has the right to choose the racing line, but may only make **one defensive move**.
-# 2.6 Blocking, weaving, or brake-checking is not allowed.

---

## 3. Track Limits

-# 3.1 At least **two wheels must remain within the track limits** at all times.
-# 3.2 Kerbs are considered part of the track unless stated otherwise.
-# 3.3 Repeated corner cutting or gaining time off track may result in penalties.

---

## 4. Overtaking Rules

-# 4.1 The overtaking driver must have **significant overlap** before the braking zone to claim the corner.
-# 4.2 Divebombs and unrealistic moves are not allowed.
-# 4.3 The defending driver must leave room if the attacking car is alongside.

---

## 5. Rejoining the Track

-# 5.1 Rejoin the track **safely and predictably** after going off.
-# 5.2 Do not rejoin into the racing line if other cars are approaching.
-# 5.3 Causing a collision while rejoining may result in penalties.

---

## 6. Yellow Flags & Incidents

-# 6.1 Slow down and avoid incidents under yellow flag conditions.
-# 6.2 Do not overtake cars involved in an accident unless it is safe and unavoidable.
-# 6.3 If you cause a minor collision, you are expected to **wait and give the position back** when safe.

---

## 7. Lobby & Race Etiquette

-# 7.1 Be in the lobby on time for qualifying and race start.
-# 7.2 Follow host instructions at all times.
-# 7.3 Rage-quitting or deliberately disrupting the lobby is not allowed.

---

## 8. Qualifying Rules
